Our background

Since 1973, Limnos Bakers has dedicated itself to producing pastries and baking goods of the highest quality. Our selection offers a broad array of products, from hand-crafted, classic favourites to trend-setting flavour combinations, drawing inspiration and recipes from around the world. we maintain a reliable methods and make every recipe from scratch with the finest and freshest ingredients.

Limnos Bakers, are runned and owned by The three Myrmingos brothers, Nick, Tasso, and Vasili. we are known for our excellence, flavour, and passion towards our business.

The bakery's guiding principle is straightforward: we create great cakes, pastries, and desserts by using premium ingredients and time-honored techniques.

What our baker is about

The finest and most freshly produced pastries and cakes are what Limnos Bakers takes great satisfaction in producing. To ensure the best quality in taste and appearance, its pastry chefs follow recipes that have been improved over time. Cakes made by Limnos Bakers with unique care and attention have an enthusiastic base of loyal consumers.

To produce delicious works of art, Limnos Bakers combines creativity with an understanding of traditional techniques. Every delicacy, including pastries, cakes, and sweets, is handcrafted from scratch using only organic ingredients. The foundation principle of our family-run bakery and pastry shop is that cakes and pastries should taste as delicious as they look. When it comes to using only the freshest ingredients, Limnos Bakers has maintained its commitment.
